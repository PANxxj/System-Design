### Self-Assessment Questions

**After Fundamentals:**
1. Can you explain when to scale vertically vs horizontally?
2. What's the difference between reliability and availability?
3. How do you choose between SQL and NoSQL?

**After Low-Level Design:**
1. Can you apply SOLID principles consistently?
2. When would you use the Observer pattern?
3. How do you design for testability?

**After High-Level Design:**
1. How do you handle data consistency in distributed systems?
2. What are the trade-offs of microservices?
3. How do you design for failure?

**After Advanced Topics:**
1. How do you implement chaos engineering?
2. What's your approach to multi-region deployment?
3. How do you optimize for cost at scale?
